Events Conducted

SKY DASH

Get ready to build and race drones through a challenging obstacle course, testing your flying skills and technical expertise. Join us this year for an adrenaline-pumping experience!

GOAL RUSH

This year, race your robot through an obstacle-filled course and showcase your navigation, agility, and programming skills in Goal Rush!

Bring your robot to the track and compete head-to-head with others to determine the fastest and most efficient design this year!

Pitch your Innovative ideas in this year's Ideathon and compete for recognition from industry experts.
